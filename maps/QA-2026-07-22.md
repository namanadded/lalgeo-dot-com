## Defect and fix

`File > Export Map…` and `File > Print…` were duplicate immediate calls to `window.print()`. Users received no explanation of how PDF export works, no opportunity to review project/layer/feature context, and the printed page had no explicit contract for excluding application chrome.

The fix adds a shared, focus-contained preparation journey with distinct PDF/print language, project summary, generated title/timestamp metadata, landscape print rules that isolate the map, and mobile-safe focus restoration. `test-print-export-preparation.mjs` is required by Production CI.
