Privacy Policy for Lal Geo Survey

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

Welcome to Lal Geo Survey. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use our application (“App”).

By using Lal Geo Survey, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not download or use the App.

1. Data Storage and Security

On-Device Storage
By default, all survey data—including geolocation information, photos, notes, and responses—is stored locally on your device.

Cloud Storage (Dropbox – Optional)

  • Data saved to Dropbox is stored only in your own Dropbox account
  • Lal Geo Survey does not access, view, or store your Dropbox data on any external servers
  • We do not copy, transmit, or retain your Dropbox content outside of your device and Dropbox

Third-Party Services
Dropbox is a third-party service governed by its own privacy policy. Lal Geo Survey is not responsible for Dropbox’s data handling practices.

Data Security
We strive to use commercially acceptable safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of storage—on-device or cloud-based—can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

2. Your Choices and Controls

  • Location Data: You can enable or disable location services at any time through your device settings.
  • Camera Access: Camera permissions can be managed through your device settings.
  • Dropbox Integration: Connecting Dropbox is optional. You may disconnect Dropbox at any time, and Lal Geo Survey will no longer sync data.
  • Survey Data: You have full control over your survey data and may create, edit, delete, export, or move it between on-device and Dropbox storage at any time.

3. No Account Creation

Lal Geo Survey does not require you to create an account with Lal Geo, nor do we require personal information such as your name or email address.

If you choose to use Dropbox, authentication is handled directly by Dropbox. Lal Geo Survey does not create or manage user accounts.

4. Data Accuracy and Usage

Lal Geo Survey is a data collection tool. We do not verify, validate, or analyze collected data. Users are solely responsible for the accuracy of the data collected and how it is used.

5. Children’s Privacy

Lal Geo Survey is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or on this page. Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
